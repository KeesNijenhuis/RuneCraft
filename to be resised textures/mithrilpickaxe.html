The mithril pickaxe is a pickaxe that is stronger than the steel pickaxe, but weaker than the adamant pickaxe. It requires an Attack level of 20 to wield, and a Mining level of 21 to mine with. It can be used in combat or in mining to mine rocks. It is also one of the lightest pickaxes in the game, making it ideal for activities such as Runecrafting via the Abyss.

Players can purchase this pickaxe for 1,300gp at Nurmof's Pickaxe Shop in the Dwarven Mine.
